gavel Official Rules

Official rules for the AI-Driven Smart Finance Hackathon 2025

apartment 1. Organizer

These Official Rules ("Rules") govern the operation of the AI-Driven Smart Finance Hackathon ("Contest" or "Hackathon"). Techno Brain Group is the Contest organizer ("Organizer").

description 2. Definitions

In these Rules, "Techno Brain," "we," "our," and "us" refer to the Organizer and "you" and "yourself" refers to a Contest participant. "Team" refers to a group of up to four (4) Contest participants working together to produce a single entry.

By entering, you agree to be bound by these Rules.

calendar_month 3. Entry Period

how_to_reg Registration: Now open until December 1, 2025
upload_file Submission: December 2, 2025 – December 8, 2025 (11:59 PM EAT)
celebration Hackathon Day: December 13, 2025

verified_user 4. Eligibility

To enter, you must be eighteen (18) years of age or older.

groups Team Requirements

  • Teams: Each Team must be comprised of no more than four (4) Team Members.
  • Team Members: Each member must be 18 years of age or older. Each Team Member may only be a member of one (1) Team.
  • Team Leaders: Each Team must designate one Team Leader who is responsible for project submission and communications with organizers.
  • Submissions: Only one (1) Entry per Team may be submitted.

Employees and directors of Techno Brain and its subsidiaries, affiliates, and persons involved in the execution or administration of this promotion are not eligible.

login 5. How to Enter

No Purchase Necessary.

To enter, visit our registration page to register your team. During the submission period (December 2-8), submit your project via GitHub Issues following the submission guidelines.

  • There is an overall limit of 1 entry per team
  • You can only be a member of one team
  • Only one person from a team needs to submit the project, with all team member names included
  • Use of any automated system to participate is prohibited

checklist 6. Eligible Entry Requirements

To be eligible, an entry must meet the following requirements:

task_alt Your submission must include:

  • Your entry must be your own original work
  • The video portion must be solely your own work (filming, editing, etc.)
  • Your entry cannot have been selected as a winner in any other contest
  • You must have obtained all consents, approvals, or licenses required

Video Requirements

The video portion of your entry must be no more than five (5) minutes in length and include:

  • A demonstration of your solution
  • A description of your experience building your entry
  • An explanation of why you chose to create this solution
  • An answer to: "How did AI help you build this solution?"

Content Restrictions

Your entry may NOT contain any content that is obscene, offensive, violent, defamatory, illegal, or that promotes illegal activities or communicates messages that may reflect negatively on the goodwill of Techno Brain.

rate_review 7. Judging Criteria

Projects will be evaluated by a panel of judges based on the following criteria:

scoreboard Scoring Breakdown

  • 20% Innovation: Is the project premise interesting and original? Is the implementation creative?
  • 20% Impact: Would this use case be valuable? Could it be utilized by organizations?
  • 20% Usability: Does it solve a real-world problem? Does it follow Responsible AI principles?
  • 20% Solution Quality: Is the repository complete with clear documentation? Is the implementation substantial?
  • 20% Category Alignment: How well does the project fit its chosen category?

emoji_events 8. Prizes

Prizes will be announced soon. Stay tuned for exciting rewards for winning teams!

Each team can only win in one category. All participants who submit a valid project will receive a certificate of participation.

Your entry may be posted on public websites and used for promotional purposes. You will not receive any compensation or credit for use of your entry beyond what is described in these Official Rules.
